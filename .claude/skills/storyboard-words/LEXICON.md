# Binder symbol lexicon

Working decodings for the storyboard ideograms. Statuses: **confirmed** (by
the author), **provisional** (Claude's reading, awaiting correction),
**unresolved**. Update this file every round; never silently re-guess a
confirmed entry.

|---|---|---|

| 13:28 | José Argüelles' Mayan-calendar decryption | 13 months to a solar year, 28 days to a month — the natural order. |

Not an individual choice; the whole society is mid-crossing. The load-bearing pair of the boards. |
