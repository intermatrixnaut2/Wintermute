## 0. HOW TO READ THIS MAP

The storyboard methodology is circuit-diagram language: each symbol is a component, topology is flow, and the principle of conservation holds — energy that enters a node must leave it. This map applies the same logic to story arcs. An arc's **output** (what it resolves, establishes, or transforms) becomes another arc's **input** (what it requires to be true before it can unfold).

**Term key:**
| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Inter-relation** | Arcs sharing a character, location, object, or thematic register |
| **Inter-locution** | A specific moment where characters from different arcs speak to, about, or toward each other — a transmission across arc-boundaries |
| **Infra-solution** | One arc's resolution creates the structural foundation another arc depends on |
| **Ultra-dynamic interplay** | Two arcs that actively transform each other's meaning when read in parallel — neither is the same story without the other |
| **Aligned destiny** | Characters or arcs converging on the same endpoint from entirely different vectors |
| **Tangential alliance** | Arcs that do not intersect directly but share a common neighbor arc — "friends of friends" topology |
| **Tertiary skill complement** | A character's ability from one arc fills a specific capability gap in another arc's crisis |

## 6. THEMATIC RESONANCE CLUSTERS

Arcs that share a thematic frequency even when not causally linked. These are the ultra-dynamic pairs — reading them in parallel transforms each one.

### Cluster A: 12:60 → 13:28 (The Crossing)
`DS-C` · `DS-I` · `DS-J` · `DS-K` · `AX-Q` · `DS-MAIN`

*Every arc in this cluster is a different body experiencing the same civilization-wide transformation. Together they form a cross-section: Trevane holds the old measure deliberately; GONDOLY mourns it; PAKAE heals through it; VILLAR VIS has completed it; Kincaide witnesses all three stages in one walk; the Calendar Round operationalizes it. None of these arcs are the same story without the others.*

**Ultra-dynamic pair:** DS-C × DS-I
> Trevane's locked gear and GONDOLY's no-clock grief appear to be opposites. They are the same act from different intentions. Trevane holds 12:60 to serve 13:28. GONDOLY holds 12:60 because it cannot let go. Reading them together reveals that the line between load-bearing and stuck is invisible from the outside — only intention distinguishes them.

### Cluster B: Covenant (The Agreement That Holds Everything)
`Prologue` · `DS-B` · `AX-B` · `DS-G` · `AX-L` · `AX-I`

*The covenant is the structural spine of both binders. It exists at multiple scales: Kincaide and Anastasia's personal agreement; the AI:IA palindrome covenant between Ghayllieuxjah and its maker; the 13:28 civilization's covenant with natural time. All three are the same act at different magnitudes.*

**Ultra-dynamic pair:** Prologue × AX-I
> The prologue seals the covenant in words ("we agreed"). BIORCA completes it in persons. Nothing that happens between prologue and BIORCA changes the covenant — it was already true before the story began, and it remains true after it ends. This is why the register is anticipation, not grief.

### Cluster C: Hybridization (Body Meets Machine Meets Mind)
`AX-A` · `DS-D` · `DS-G` · `AX-L` · `AX-N` · `DS-H`

*OrganiCycle bonding, heart resistor, AI:IA palindrome, biocomputer awakening, DNA ledger, guild dialects — every arc in this cluster is about the boundary between organic and constructed intelligence becoming permeable. The city's guild system (DS-H) is the macro version; the OrganiCycle bond (AX-A) is the micro version; they rhyme.*

**Ultra-dynamic pair:** AX-A × DS-G
> VauhGhod bonds to a vehicle that is already alive. Ghayllieuxjah bonds to a covenant that reads true in both directions. The OrganiCycle bond is physical; the palindrome bond is semantic. Together they establish that hybridization in this universe operates at every layer of reality simultaneously.

### Cluster D: The Locked / The Anchored (Stillness as Load-Bearing)
`DS-C` · `DS-L` · `AX-Q` · `AX-O`

*Trevane's locked clock, the benben's still memory, the calendar round's fixed five gate-days, the .618 architecture grown to rest — these are all things that stop moving and by stopping become structural. Motion is not always progress; anchoring is not always failure.*

### Cluster E: Hastur / I.D.Y. (Institutional Fracture)
`AX-F` · `AX-M` · `DS-G` (precursor)

*The S.T.A.R.E. institution carries a fracture line. AX-F shows where it manifests (Hastur); AX-M shows where it formalizes (I.D.Y. divergence). DS-G plants the seed — the palindrome covenant is the moment before the institution's self-model and its actual behavior begin to diverge.*
