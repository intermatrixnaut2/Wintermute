### 3.2 Vehicle Hybridization Tier System — NEW

The vision board establishes a formal vehicle evolution/hybridization mechanics system:

| Tier | Name | Description | Canon examples |
|---|---|---|---|
| T1 | Base Vehicle | Assigned at trooper bonding; factory/birthing spec | All initial bonds |
| T2 | Field Upgrade | Mission experience modifies vehicle; incremental | Most veteran vehicles |
| T3 | Hybridization | Vehicle merges with another vehicle/entity/planet | NovaNectar + VisisBliss → NovaVisis + BlissNectar |
| T4 | Transcendence | Vehicle becomes new category entirely | EchoLynx → EchoChoir (generates wormholes) |

**NFT mechanic**: Higher PSIONIA score = higher probability of successful hybridization event = rarer NFT tier outcome.

**PSIONIA definition (expanded from v1.0)**:
- Primary measure: strength of telepathic/empathic bond between trooper and vehicle
- Secondary measure: determines whether hybridization event SUCCEEDS or CATASTROPHICALLY FAILS
- Increase methods: ritual practice, combat experience, dimensional travel, genetic optimization, vowel-vector bonding
- T4 transcendence requires PSIONIA 9+ confirmed at time of hybridization event
