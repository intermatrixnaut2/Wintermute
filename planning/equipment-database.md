## 4. ARMOR SYSTEMS

### 4A. Tachyonic Carapace (House PolyMatriFoci)

The definitive armor class for long-crossing OrganiCycles. Full spec in Section 2A above (HPM entry). Key principles:

1. **Grown, not built.** Carapace plates grow around the specific vehicle-pilot-mind matrix. They cannot be interchanged between vehicles.
2. **Four-fold agreement.** Carapace · Creature · Man · Made Mind must all agree before the armor transfers at the GODGATE.
3. **Seven-plate series.** The full series would entomb the bearer. The weighing determines the carry-count. Never one plate more.
4. **The seventh plate is a covenant.** It is not discarded; it is held. It will be needed at the far gate — the seventh plate's story function is the gift-office of Scene 14 / Open Socket OS-4.
5. **Spectral progression.** Plates are arrayed Red → Violet in ascending ECR contribution. The violet plate (nearest the spine) is always the last fitted and the first to ring when the bearer speaks truth.
